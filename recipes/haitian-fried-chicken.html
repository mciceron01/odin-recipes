Ingredients

      3 pounds chicken legs
      1 cup lemon juice
      ½ white onion, chopped½ white onion, chopped
      1 head garlic, cloves separated and minced
      3 tablespoons seasoned salt
      3 tablespoons paprika
      3 tablespoons ground thyme
      2 tablespoons ground black pepper
      2 tablespoons whole cloves
      vegetable oil for frying

Directions

Step 1

  1. Combine chicken legs, lemon juice, onion, garlic, seasoned salt, paprika, thyme, pepper, and cloves in a large bowl.
  2. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 12 hours.

Step 2

  1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan over medium-high heat.
